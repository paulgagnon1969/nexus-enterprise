Chapter 1: Welcome to Nexus Contractor Connect

The All-in-One Platform with Full Project Management Capabilities for Construction & Restoration

Construction and restoration projects demand precision, coordination, and visibility from the first bid to final payment. Fragmented tools create silos that lead to miscommunication, cost overruns, rework, and delayed payments.

Nexus Contractor Connect (NCC) eliminates this by combining a contractor marketplace with full end-to-end project management ‚Äî all in one secure, cloud-native workspace.

Gone are the days when you have to have multiple logins for the same application to work for different people.

With NCC's unique "Collaborator Technology", you can invite multiple subcontractors (and other stakeholders) to collaborate across one project ‚Äî seamlessly, securely, and without forcing them to juggle separate accounts, portals, or logins.

Everyone works from the same unified workspace: real-time updates, shared files, tasks, logs, and visibility ‚Äî tailored precisely by role.

Project Owner vs. Collaborator Roles ‚Äì At a Glance

Project OwnerCollaborator (e.g., Subcontractors, Trades, Partners)
Full control: Create/edit/delete projectScoped access: View/edit only their scope (tasks, logs, time, assets)
Invite/remove collaborators & set permissionsReal-time contribution: Daily logs, photos, time entries, change requests
Set budgets, baselines, approvalsNo access to unrelated financials/other subs' data (unless shared)
Access all reports, financials, adminJoin via email/invite link ‚Äî secure NCC login for full role
Owns tenant/company dataLeave/remove without affecting other projects

Chapter 2: Signing Up, Signing In, and Surface Navigation

Goal: Get inside your personalized workspace securely in under 2‚Äì3 minutes.

Step-by-Step: Creating an Account

  1. Visit https://nexus-enterprise-web.vercel.app (or your custom domain).
  2. Click Sign Up on the landing page.
  3. Enter email, strong password, company name (creates your first tenant).
  4. Verify email link ‚Üí complete quick profile (trade/specialty, optional logo).

Signing In (Daily Use)

  1. Enter email + password.
  2. Optional 2FA for team security.
  3. Language toggle (EN/ES) available on login screen.

Main Interface Layout

  • Left Sidebar (collapsible): Dashboard, Projects, Estimating, Schedule, Daily Logs, Time, Assets, Marketplace, Reports, Admin, Help
  • Top Bar: Search, Notifications, Profile, Quick + button
  • Main Area: Responsive content area

First Actions After Login

  • Dashboard loads automatically (Chapter 3)
  • Follow onboarding tour (if present)
  • Invite teammates from Profile ‚Üí Team

Tip: Use the sidebar to navigate ‚Äî it's your main map of the platform.
