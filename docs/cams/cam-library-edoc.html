What is a CAM?

A Competitive Advantage Module (CAM) is a distinct capability within the Nexus platform that no competitor offers — or offers only partially. Each CAM is evaluated on four dimensions (1–10 scale each, 40 max):

DimensionQuestion
UniquenessDo competitors have this? (1 = common, 10 = unique to Nexus)
ValueHow much does this help users? (1 = minor, 10 = critical)
DemonstrabilityCan we show this in a live demo? (1 = hard, 10 = easy)
DefensibilityIs this hard to replicate? (1 = easy, 10 = hard)

Key Metrics

  • NexOP — Operating Percentage: the share of a contractor's annual revenue that a module recovers, protects, or avoids losing. Expressed as a percentage for instant comparability at any company size.
  • NexINT — Operational Integrity: a composite index measuring financial accuracy, process completion, compliance, and data quality. Industry baseline ~72%; Nexus target ~95%.

FIN-ACC-0001 — NexVERIFY: Multi-Source Expense Convergence

"Two sources. One truth. Zero duplicates. Every dollar verified."

Problem: When the same purchase is captured from multiple sources (receipt + CC charge), every other system either misses the duplicate (inflating costs by 2×) or deletes it (losing the audit trail).

NCC Advantage: Detects convergence via fuzzy vendor matching (11 merchant families), keeps both records in a linked sibling group, applies a GAAP-clean verification offset that zeros out the duplicate's financial impact while preserving an unbreakable audit chain. Arrival-order-agnostic.
