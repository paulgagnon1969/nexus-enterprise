### NCC Software Transparency & Project Management

Nexus Contractor Connect (NCC) software provides real-time transparency for all stakeholders, tracking build progress, weekly expenditures vs. total cost outlay, and variances. The Project Estimate Task List (PETL) drives scheduling for every room, unit, and phase, with daily updates at granular levels (room/unit/building).

**How NCC Works for Investors:**

<div class="mermaid">
flowchart LR
    PM[PM Updates Progress] --> PETL[PETL Task Tracker]
    PETL --> DASH[Investor Dashboard]
    MAT[Materials Allocation] --> RECON[Weekly Reconciliation]
    RECON --> DASH
    DASH --> ALERT[Variance Alerts]
    DASH --> PROG[Build Progress %]
    DASH --> COST[Cost vs. Budget]
</div>

**Example:** If the PM marks sheetrock hanging 100% complete in units 1–16 but only 30% in 17–24, the dashboard reflects this instantly. By tying materials allocation to real costs, NCC reconciles weekly to flag productivity/cost variances, ensuring maximum efficiency and accountability.

### Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Entitlements / rezoning delays (PD approval, annexation) | Medium | Medium | Pre-application meetings with Comal County/New Braunfels staff; dedicated entitlement consultant; phased approach allows Building 1 start during review |
| Construction cost overruns (materials/labor volatility) | Low-Medium | Medium | Fixed-price GMP contracts; 10% hard-cost contingency; Nexus CMS vertical integration + real-time NCC gauges/PETL updates to flag variances weekly |
| Lease-up delays (market absorption slower than projected) | Medium | Medium | Conservative 94% occupancy assumption; workforce/garden-style focus in high-demand corridor; phased build allows cash flow from early buildings to support later |
| Interest rate / refi market shifts (higher rates reduce LTV proceeds) | Low | High | Phased refi strategy spreads exposure; conservative 6.5% cap rate assumption; interest rate cap on debt; strong submarket fundamentals (slowing supply, +2.8% pop growth) |
| Sequential phasing risks (delays cascade to later buildings) | Medium | Medium | Real-time NCC tracking for cash-flow/operational efficiency; contingency reserves; Building 1 as larger Style B for stronger initial refi trigger; PETL daily updates |
