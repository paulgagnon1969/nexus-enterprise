# Schumann Project — 46x36 The Lean Cottage
## Scope of Work & Materials Takeoff

### Building Summary
- **Total Living SF:** 1,540
- **Porch/Deck:** 485 SF
- **2-Car Garage:** 576 SF
- **Max Height:** 16'-1"
- **Footprint:** 46'-0" x 36'-0" (house) + 24'-6" garage wing
- **Roof Pitch:** 2:12
- **Structure:** Light timber framing
- **Bedrooms:** 3 (Master, BR#2, BR#3)
- **Bathrooms:** 2 (Master Bath, Bath #2)
- **Rooms:** Kitchen, Dining, Living Room, Hallway, Mud Room, Pantry, 3x WIC

---

## DIVISION 1 — GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

### 1.1 Permits & Inspections
- Building permit
- Footing inspection (prior to concrete pour)
- Framing inspection
- Electrical rough-in inspection
- Plumbing rough-in inspection
- Mechanical rough-in inspection
- Insulation inspection
- Final inspection

### 1.2 Site Conditions
- Soil bearing capacity assumed at 2,000 PSF (no soils report — verify before foundation work)
- Radon testing required per state/local laws
- Termite classification: Very Heavy — soil treatment required
- Frost line depth: 12"
- Wind design speed: 115 MPH
- Seismic design category: D1

---

## DIVISION 2 — SITE WORK & EXCAVATION

### 2.1 Scope
- Site clearing and grading (by civil engineer's drawings — not included in this set)
- Excavation for continuous footings and pier footings
- Backfill and compaction (95% max density per ASTM D698, 6"-8" lifts)
- Grade to slope away from building at 2% minimum
- No footings on frozen or soft earth

### 2.2 Materials
- Clean compacted fill (no organics, stones, debris)
- Termite soil treatment — toxicant with 5-year guarantee, entire footprint + 2' beyond perimeter

## NOTES FOR ESTIMATOR

1. **Quantities are approximate** — field verification and detailed quantity survey recommended before bidding.
2. **No HVAC design included** in this drawing set — mechanical contractor must provide design, equipment selection, and duct layout.
3. **No plumbing layout** included — plumber to design per code from fixture locations shown on floor plan.
4. **Soil report not provided** — foundation design assumes 2,000 PSF. Verify with geotechnical engineer before proceeding.
5. **Basement option** significantly increases scope — estimate separately.
6. **Owner selections needed** for: flooring types, cabinet/countertop spec, appliances, fixture finishes, exterior siding color, roofing color.
7. **Site work, excavation, grading, landscaping** are NOT included in this drawing set — per civil engineer's scope.
8. **Energy code compliance** should be verified for local jurisdiction — insulation values (R-19 walls, R-38 roof, R-30 floor) meet general requirements but local amendments may apply.
